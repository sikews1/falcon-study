Geometria

Arees, perimetres i volums de figures geometriques

1 Arees i perimetres (2D)

Quadrat (costat a)Area = a² | Perimetre = 4a
Rectangle (a × b)Area = a×b | Perimetre = 2(a+b)
Triangle (base b, altura h)Area = (b×h)/2 | Perimetre = a+b+c
Cercle (radi r)Area = πr² | Perimetre = 2πr
Exemple: Rectangle 5×3 → Area = 15, Perimetre = 16

2 Volums (3D)

Cub (aresta a)Volum = a³
Prisma rectangular (a×b×c)Volum = a×b×c
Cilindre (radi r, altura h)Volum = πr²h
Exemple: Cub d'aresta 4 → Volum = 64
